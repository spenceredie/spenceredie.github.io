Tappy Bird

I published this game over on Unity Play before I lost the files to a fire. If the player doesn't load for whatever reason, you can see the game over at Unity Play.

A rite of passage, the Flappy Bird clone. I made this to practice batching building for WebGL. My daring innovation was to include a dash - move the cursor to the right before letting go and the bird will do a short dash.

I made all the sounds for this game using Bfxr.
