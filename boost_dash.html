Boost Dash

Proof of concept for a game about mastering movement. It's made to feel clunky at first but get smoother as you learn to control your character.
Works best with a controller.

Controls:

A & D or left stick to move left and right
Space or XBox A to jump
K or XBox B to "Step"
Hold J or XBox Rb to "Dash"
S or XBox Lb to "Dive"
Left Mouse Click or XBox X to attack
