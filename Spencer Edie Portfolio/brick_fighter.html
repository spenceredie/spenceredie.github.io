Tappy Bird

This was my first attempt at publishing a WebGL build, and as you'll see it's pretty scuffed. If the player doesn't load for whatever reason, you can see the game over at Unity Play.

The game is based on a typical brick breaker game, but instead of being a paddle you play as a character who runs around hitting the ball. You can run, jump, wall-jump, hit the ball, and hit it multiple times to power it up. Destroying all the bricks in each level will take you to the next.

I made all the sounds for this game using Bfxr.

Gamepad compatible.

Controls:

WASD / left stick to move
Space / A to jump
Left Mouse / X to hit the ball (hold to aim)
