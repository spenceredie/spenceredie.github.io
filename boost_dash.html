Boost Dash

Proof of concept for a game about mastering movement. It's made to feel clunky at first but get smoother as you learn to control your character.
Works best with a controller.

Controls:

A & D or left stick to move left and right
W or XBox A to jump
Space or XBox B to "Step"
Hold Left Shift or XBox Rb to "Dash" left/right/up
S or XBox Lb to "Dive"
J or XBox X to attack

Instructions:

The meter in the bottom right is your "boost gauge." if you run out you'll be exhausted for a moment.
Jump right after landing a Dive to do a super jump. The sooner you jump the higher you'll go.
Dash during a "Step" to bypass the long Dash startup. Dive or Jump to end a Dash and you'll avoid the long recovery.
Using "Step" will leave an afterimage which draws enemy fire.
Destroying enemies refills your boost gauge.
